About the Mod

GTA San Andreas Stories, also known as GTA San Andreas Stories: The Return of Ryder or GTA San Andreas Stories: 1987, is a fan-made, work-in-progress mod by babamohammed2022 that aims to recreate the vibe of the GTA Stories series.

The story utilizes DYOM 8.1 and follows Ryder as he returns to San Andreas in 1987, encountering new adventures and reconnecting with old friends.

For more information and a FAQ, join our Discord Server.

Disclaimer: If you are using the Essential Modpack, please delete the Widescreen mod from Modloader to avoid game crashes on startup. Additionally, be aware that SAMP and the Widescreen mod are incompatible with the modpack.
